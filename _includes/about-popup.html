Q: How do I install these mods?

A: Each mod has its own download link and installation instructions on its individual page. Generally, you'll need to extract the files and follow a couple of simple steps. Make sure to install the Essential Files first found at the top of the page.

Q: Do I need the original S.T.A.L.K.E.R. games?

A: Standalone mods don't require the original games, but check each project's features. It's best practice to own the original games before starting your modding adventures.

Q: Are these mods safe to download?
