Q: How do I install these mods?

A: Each mod has its own download link and installation instructions on its individual page. Generally, you'll need to extract the files and follow a couple of simple steps. Make sure to install the Essential Files first found at the top of the page. If you have any issues, refer to the Tips & Troubleshooting section, and the readme.txt found directly in the archive you downloaded.

Q: Do I need the original S.T.A.L.K.E.R. games to play these mods?

A: Standalone mods, by definition, don't require the original games, but check each project's features to be sure of any requirements. It's best practice however to own the original games and have them played before starting your modding adventures.

Q: Are these mods safe to download?

A: The short answer is yes, all mods are curated and tested. However, I do not control all the links / mirrors so I can't vouch for anything happening outside of this website. Always exercise caution when downloading files from unknown sources.

Q: How does the rating system work?

A: Ratings are an average of the grades found on moddb.com, ap-pro.ru and stalkermod.ru, weighted by the number of votes on each website. It's a good way to reflect the overall community opinion of the mod's quality, story, gameplay, and technical polish. Keep in mind that this is a curated list though, and all featured mods are worth playing regardless of rating.

Q: Can I request a mod to be added to the archive?

A: Not right now, I'm still working on a big backlog of mods I want to play / review and add to the website. But at some point yes, I'll make an announcement when I need new and fresh recommendations!
